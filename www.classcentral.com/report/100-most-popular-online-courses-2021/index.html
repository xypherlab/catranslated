It’s been nine years since a few Stanford professors decided to offer their courses online for free. These later became known as MOOCs, for भारी मात्रा में खुले ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम. And 2012 became the ‘MOOC का वर्ष’.

Well, 2020 might be MOOCs’ next most consequential year. The COVID-19 pandemic has boosted learners’ interest in online education. MOOC providers, in particular, have seen a significant traffic influx, as previously क्लास सेंट्रल की रिपोर्ट द्वारा कवर किया गया.

The pandemic has also impacted the most popular list. Compared to last year’s 100 सबसे लोकप्रिय पाठ्यक्रम, the number of स्वास्थ्य और चिकित्सा ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम has gone up.

In fact, 18 courses in the ranking are directly related to COVID. The top course, with over 1 million enrollments, is Johns Hopkins’ Covid-19 संपर्क अनुरेखण. It is followed by Harvard’s कोविड -19 के लिए यांत्रिक वेंटिलेशन, with 300K enrollments.

15K नामांकन के आसपास रैंकिंग के निचले भाग में पाठ्यक्रम।संयुक्त, ये 100 मुफ्त ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम 6 मिलियन नामांकन के लिए खाते हैं।
